Introducing

DirectTorque X20

A 20Nm direct drive wheelbase. Built with the spirit of DIY. Powered by OpenFFBoard. Experience precision and control like never before.

The RSR team goal is to elevate the immersion of racing simulation. We deliver the excellence yet affordable sim racing experience by supplying both quality hardware and endless innovations. What began as the enthusiasm of passionate DIY builders and sim racers has transformed into a well-engineered product for serious and pro sim racing gamers like you.

RSR DT X20 is powered by refurbished Dorna 130DNA servo motor. We have carefully selected and tested each unit to make sure it meets our quality standard. The 20 Nm peak torque is giving us a wide headroom for feedback detail. We also upgraded the encoder with a high resolution magnetic encoder (16384ppr). Whether you want the feedback detail to be smooth as butter or sharp as knife, so be it.

To have full control of innovation and improvements, we designed and iterated our own PCBs that are exclusively built and engineered for RSR DT product lines. This enable us to transform the servo motor into a sleek appearance by eliminating the bulky power and encoder sockets. No more messy cables.

We owe a great deal to the free and open-source software that helped us reach where we are today. We have paired OpenFFBoard firmware with our RSR Paddock software to deliver easy way to control your wheelbase. We are keen to giving back to community by contributing back to OpenFFBoard and open sourced our own software.

Product Features:

  • Peak Torque: 20 Nm
  • Compatible with a wide range of games
  • USB type C port for communication
